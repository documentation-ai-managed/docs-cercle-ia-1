## Overview

Creating a new documentation project in Cercle IA is simple.

## Steps

1. Log in to your Cercle IA dashboard.
2. Click **"New Project"** on the homepage.
3. Enter your project name and optional description.
4. Click **"Create"**.
5. Your project appears in the workspace list.

**Tip:** Organize docs in folders for clarity.
