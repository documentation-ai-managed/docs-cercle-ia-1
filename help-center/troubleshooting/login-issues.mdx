---
title: Can't log in to Cercle IA
description: Quick fixes for common login problems.
---

## Symptoms
- Login page reloads with no error
- Wrong password or email message

## Quick Fixes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Double-check your email and password. Use the show-password toggle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot password" and follow the email instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    If issues persist, clear cache and try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
