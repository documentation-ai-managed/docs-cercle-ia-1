## Overview
Easily set up your first Cercle IA documentation project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Projects">
    In your Cercle IA dashboard, click on **Projects** in the top menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Press the **New Project** button and fill in project details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Docs">
    Start adding pages or folders from your new project dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
