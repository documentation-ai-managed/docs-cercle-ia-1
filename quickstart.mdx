## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An active email address for account verification
- Access to the beta waitlist at [cercle-gpt.com/#waitlist](https://cercle-gpt.com/#waitlist)
</Callout>

Review these requirements to avoid setup issues.

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to join the beta and set up your Cercle IA account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Waitlist" icon="users">
    Visit the [beta waitlist page](https://cercle-gpt.com/#waitlist).

    Fill out the form with your email and details.

    Await approval (typically within 24 hours).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Cercle IA.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Log in at [app.cercle-gpt.com/login](https://app.cercle-gpt.com/login).

    Set your preferences for models like GPT, Claude, and Gemini.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In to the Dashboard

<Tabs default-tab="1">
  <Tab title="Existing Account" icon="log-in">
    Navigate to [app.cercle-gpt.com/login](https://app.cercle-gpt.com/login).

    Enter your email and password.

    Enable two-factor authentication for added security.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Forgot Password" icon="key">
    Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page.

    Enter your email to receive a reset link.

    Follow the instructions to create a new password.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Make Your First Multi-Model Query

Test Cercle IA's power by comparing responses across AIs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Your Prompt" icon="edit">
    In the query box, type a question like: `Compare the best practices for writing a research paper using different AI models.`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Models" icon="zap">
    Choose 2-5 models: GPT-5.1, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity.

    Toggle `Private GPT` for confidential data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Comparison" icon="play">
    Click "Compare Responses".

    Review side-by-side outputs and select the best one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Share" icon="save">
    Star your favorite response.

    Export results or share via link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Pro tip: Start with broad queries to see model strengths, then refine for specific tasks like code or analysis.
</Callout>
